How to play

Guess today's Brawler from Supercell's mobile game Brawl Stars. Changes every 24 hours.

To begin, simply type in the name of a Brawler. Pressing enter, clicking the button on the screen, or clicking a suggestion will all submit your guess.

The color of the resulting tiles indicates how close you were to the answer.
